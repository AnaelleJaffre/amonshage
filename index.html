How I work

If you would like to commission me, you can ask me anything you want to know via various contact methods:

  • By mail at osany.art@gmail.com
  • By message on Artstation
  • By message on Instagram

PS : If you send me a request by mail, make sure you put an object, present yourself correctly, formulate a coherent demand and sign. Please use a professional adress with your real name into it. If this is not respected, I will consider it as spam and never answer you.


Then, I will ask you precisely about the content you would like me to draw. I will also need you to specify the style.

Don't hesitate to refer to some of the artworks you see here in my portfolio, it will help me understand your request better¬†üòä.

Three parts will then follow:

  1. First, I will show you different sketches and you will choose the one you prefer;
  2. Then, I will complete it, taking your requests into account;
  3. Finally, you will be free to ask me to adjust any details you would like to change.
