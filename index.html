How I work

If you would like to commission me, you can ask me everything you want to know via various contact methods:

  • By mail at osany.art@gmail.com
  • By message on Artstation
  • By message on Instagram

Then, I will ask you precisely what is the content you would like me to draw. I will also need you to specify the style.

Don't hesitate to refer to some of the artworks you see here in my portfolio, it will help me understand you request better¬†üòä.

Three parts will then follow:

  1. First, I will offer you different sketches and you will have to choose the one you prefer;
  2. Then, I will complete it, taking your demands into accounts;
  3. Finally, you will be free to ask me to adjust any details you would like to change.
